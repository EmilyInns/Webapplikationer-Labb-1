BDZ

TWICEs 1st Japanese album

BDZ was released on September 12th 2018. The title is an abbreviation for 'Bulldozer'. It is the groups second full studio album, and their first full Japanese album. It includes five new songs and five previously released ones, including a cover of the Jackson 5s 'I Want You Back'.

Tracklist:

  1. BDZ
  2. One More Time
  3. Candy Pop
  4. L.O.V.E
  5. Wishing
  6. Say It Again
  7. Wake Me Up
  8. Brand New Girl
  9. Be as One
  10. I Want You Back
