SIGNAL

TWICEs 4th mini album

Signal was released on May 15th 2017. The lyrics for 'EYES EYES EYES' were written by Chaeyound and Jihyo, marking the first time TWICE released a song with lyrics written entirely by group members

Tracklist:

  2. 하루에 세번 THREE TIMES A DAY
  3. ONLY 너 ONLY YOU
  4. HOLD ME TIGHT
  5. EYES EYES EYES
  6. SOMEONE LIKE ME
