Merry & Happy

TWICEs 1st repackage

Merry & Happy and it's title track 'Heart Shaker' was released on December 11th 2017. The album was a repackage of Twicetagram featuring 2 new songs. It is the only TWICE album to have a music video for more than one new song, with the video for 'Merry & Happy' releasing 11 days later. The titular track is TWICEs first Christmas song.
