Summer Nights

TWICEs 2nd Special album

Summer Nights and it's title track 'Dance The Night Away' was released on July 9th 2018. The album is a repackage of What is Love, including the previously CD-only track 'STUCK' as well as three new tracks.

Tracklist:

  1. Dance The Night Away
  2. CHILLAX
  3. Shot Thru The Heart
  4. What is Love
  5. SWEET TALKER
  6. HO!
  7. DEJAVU
  8. SAY YES
  9. STUCK
