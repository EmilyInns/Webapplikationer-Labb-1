TWICEcoaster: Lane 2

TWICEs 1st special album

TWICEcoaster: Lane 2 and it's title track Knock Knock was released on October 24 2016. The album is a repackage of TWICEcoaster: Lane 1, including two new tracks as well as instrumental versions of the title tracks from The Story Begins and Page Two.

Tracklist:

  1. Knock Knock
  2. Ice Cream
  3. TT
  4. 1 to 10
  5. Ponytail
  6. Jelly Jelly
  7. Pit-a-Pat
  8. Next Page
  9. One in a million
  10. TT - Tak Remix (CD-only)
  11. Like Ooh-Ahh - Instrumental (CD-only)
  12. Cheer Up - Instrumental (CD-only)
  13. TT - Instrumental (CD-only)
