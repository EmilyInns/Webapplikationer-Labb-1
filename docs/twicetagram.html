Twicetagram

TWICEs 1st Studio album

Twicetagram and it's title track 'LIKEY' was released on October 30th 2017. This was the groups first full length album featuring 13 new songs. It was the best-selling K-pop girl group album of 2017, having sold 320,389 copies by the end of the year.
